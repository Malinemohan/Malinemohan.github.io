About Me

I am a Biotechnology graduate with hands-on experience in molecular docking, high-throughput virtual screening, ADMET profiling, and GROMACS-based MD simulation. I completed a research internship at SRIHER Chennai focused on in silico quorum sensing inhibitor discovery against oral and periodontal pathogens.

I also completed a project internship at IIT Madras on computational analysis of metabolic heterogeneity in T cells at single-cell resolution, and led the iGEM REC-Chennai 2024 team to a Silver Medal, where I worked on polymer-protein MD simulations for a microplastic biosensor project.

I deliver clean, structured PDF reports with publication-ready figures — suitable for thesis chapters, manuscripts, and grant proposals.

Past work

Portfolio Highlights

VIRTUAL SCREENING · SRIHER 2026
LuxS Inhibitor Screening — F. nucleatum
High-throughput two-round virtual screening of 15,000 phytochemicals from COCONUT, NPAtlas, and IMPPAT 2.0 against the AlphaFold2-predicted LuxS quorum sensing target in Fusobacterium nucleatum. Full ADMET profiling with Lipinski, Veber, PAINS, and hERG filters applied.
MD SIMULATION · MANUSCRIPT
Aspochalasin K — S. mutans ComD Study
Contributed to GROMACS-based MD simulation setup, system preparation, trajectory analysis, and manuscript writing for a marine-derived compound study targeting the ComD/ComE quorum sensing system in Streptococcus mutans.
MD SIMULATION · iGEM 2024
Polymer-Protein MD — PLASTICOPS
Ran polymer-protein molecular dynamics simulations as part of the iGEM REC-Chennai 2024 Silver Medal project — a synthetic biology approach to isolating microplastics from the environment using genetically engineered peptides.
MOLECULAR DOCKING · S. PYOGENES
Rgg2 Docking — Mulberry Phytochemicals
Molecular docking study of 54 GC-MS-identified phytochemicals from mulberry extract against the Rgg2 quorum sensing receptor of Streptococcus pyogenes, using AutoDock Vina with full visualisation and interaction analysis.
